Drawing on my background in both computer science and spatial data analysis, I conducted an exploratory data analysis project focusing on public health metrics, specifically youth sleep patterns.

### The Methodology
Using **Python** and **Jupyter Notebooks**, this project processes raw datasets to extract meaningful insights. 

* **Data Cleaning & Preprocessing:** Handling missing variables, normalizing data fields, and preparing the raw sleep datasets for accurate statistical modeling.
* **Statistical Analysis:** Applying core statistical principles to identify trends, correlations, and anomalies within the youth demographic.
* **Data Visualization:** Translating complex dataframes into readable charts and graphs to clearly communicate the findings.

This project highlights my ability to bridge the gap between raw data and actionable intelligence, utilizing standard Python data science libraries to tell a clear story.
